The source of the more detailed figures reported in the latter is the FAO Fisheries Circular No. 821, Rev.
[Laurenti, G. (comp.) 1961-1999. Fish and fishery products: world apparent consumption statistics based on food balance sheets], which was compiled using statistics held in the FAO fishery statistical database in August 2001. The world total data presented in the Overview (Tables 1 and 2) are based on more recent production figures of March 2002, which also incorporated historical revisions. The food fish supply data in Tables 1 and 2 are indicative and may be subject to further revision when the food balance sheets are updated.
